What our Client’s are Saying…
“Their caring attitude and attention to detail are a welcome relief when going through a difficult time.”

I first learned of Home Comfort Care when searching for a caregiver for my boss, who needed assistance following surgery. His physician’s office recommended Home Comfort Care as a perfect fit for our needs.  We ultimately employed a caregiver from Home Comfort Care and found the service to be terrific. The caregiver’s attention to my boss was first-rate and the family spoke very highly of the company.

However, it was not until my own family was in need for a caregiver that I really had a chance to work with Cheryl. My dad was placed in in-home hospice and things became more difficult for my family to manage alone. Based on my earlier experience with Home Comfort Care, I did not hesitate to contact Cheryl. Within a week, we had a plan in place and a caregiver staying with my dad for eight to ten hours per night. 

Not only are the caregivers providing much needed physical care for my dad, we also have the added expertise of Cheryl’s RN skills. I have found Cheryl to be very hands-on with the caregivers during the evening should they have any questions. She is also very responsive to my family and keeps in regular communication. 

In conclusion, I would not hesitate to recommend Home Comfort Care for you or a loved one. Their caring attitude and attention to detail are a welcome relief when going through a difficult time. You can rest easy knowing that you are in good hands.

Nancy G.
